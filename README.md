# 🏥 Smart Health Access System
**Healthcare Anytime, Anywhere**

## 📖 Project Overview

The **Smart Health Access System** is a mobile application designed to close the gap between patients and healthcare providers in communities where clinic visits are difficult to arrange, medical histories are scattered across paper files, and appointment scheduling depends on long queues or phone calls that often go unanswered.

The system gives:
- **Patients** a single place to register, search for doctors by specialty, book and track appointments, receive automated reminders, and view their own medical history.
- **Doctors** a structured way to manage their daily schedule, update patient records after consultations, and review a patient's full history before treating them.
- **Administrators** the oversight tools needed to manage users, manage doctors, and generate reports on system usage.

The system was built as a high-fidelity prototype using **React Native** for the mobile client, a **RESTful API** application layer, and **MongoDB** for data storage, following a three-tier architecture across Presentation, Application, and Database layers.

## 🌍 SDG Alignment Statement

This project is aligned with **United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3 — Good Health and Well-Being**, which calls for ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all ages, including the target of achieving **universal health coverage and access to quality essential healthcare services (Target 3.8)**.

By digitising appointment booking, medical record-keeping, and health reminders, the Smart Health Access System:

- **Reduces missed appointments** by allowing patients to book, confirm, and receive reminders through their mobile device rather than depending on walk-ins or unanswered phone calls.
- **Shortens the time between symptom recognition and care** by giving patients instant visibility into doctor availability and specialties.
- **Supports treatment adherence** through automated medication reminders, reducing the need for clinical follow-up on routine prescriptions.
- **Preserves continuity of care** by giving doctors access to a patient's full medical history before every consultation, even in high-turnover clinic settings.
- **Empowers patients with data ownership** through downloadable PDF medical records that can be carried to any clinic, removing dependency on a single provider's paper files.

Released under the **MIT Licence**, the system is freely reusable by other developers, clinics, and NGOs facing the same access-to-healthcare problem — making it a contribution toward equitable digital health infrastructure in low-resource settings.
